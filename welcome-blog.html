Welcome to my blog.

2 JUNE 2018

Hello! I am Amanshu Raikwar and this is my personal Blog. For a long time, I have wanted to make a website of my own, which reflects me as a person and as a professional. This is my take on it.

Design of this website is kept minimal and bold. The main navigation of this website is distributed across three colors - red, orange and green. This gives a sense to the user what page he/she is currently on. Basic design of this website is kept minimal and sometimes a little too bold. White color is used as a background to give a feel that the user is reading on a real paper. I have experimented with various shades of Gray to know about the limits of using the shades of a single color. I have also experimented with typography to create a heirarchy of importance of text.

Although this is not perfect but I will keep experimenting to keep the design clean. This is my playground where I can play without rules. I hope you enjoy my work.

- Amanshu Raikwar
